Why Should I Open a Checking Account at CFS?

All personal checking accounts are not equal. And whether you're getting your first checking account, or just opening a new one, a CFS personal checking account offers you a variety of benefits, including flexibility and a high return on your money. Additional benefits you'll enjoy when you get a checking account at CFS, depending on which type of personal checking account you choose, include:


  • Low opening balance requirements
  • Low or no minimum balance requirements and monthly fees
  • Free Visa® debit card
  • Free online bill payment
  • Free telephone banking
  • Unlimited access to over 32,000 ATMs in the CFS, Allpoint® and Presto® networks throughout the U.S.
  • Security of dealing with an FDIC-insured institution

What should I consider before I open a checking account at CFS?

Checking accounts are not one size fits all. Before you get a new checking account, you should determine your personal checking style by asking important questions, such as:


  • What minimum balance can I maintain comfortably?
  • How many checks do I write per month?
  • How often do I use the ATM machine?
  • Do I need my checks returned?
  • How often do I bounce a check?

Are online banking and bill payment options important to you? Once you have these answers, it should be easy to determine which CFS personal checking account is best tailored to your needs.
