What Is a No-Fee Checking Account?

Free or no-fee checking accounts are those that do not impose any maintenance or activity fees. According to Informa Research Services, the number of financial institutions offering free or no-fee checking accounts has increased to nearly 60 percent ? up over 30 percent since 2001. But not all no-fee checking accounts are really free. Sometimes you're required to keep a minimum balance, or you may incur fees for transactions with a teller or through an ATM machine.

Open a no-fee checking account at CFS

CFS no-fee checking accounts don't require you to maintain a specific minimum daily balance; there are no monthly maintenance fees; and you can open a free checking account with just a small deposit. When you open a no-fee checking account at CFS, you'll enjoy a variety of benefits, including:


  • No minimum balance
  • Unlimited check writing
  • Free online banking
  • Free online bill-pay
  • Free funds transfers in online banking
  • 24-hour telephone banking
  • ATM/debit card (upon credit approval)

In addition, you have the security of dealing with an FDIC-insured institution. We're confident you'll be happy with your CFS checking account, and may even consider it the best free checking account you've ever had.
